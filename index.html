Wellness is a critical investment

Employee health is business health

In the UK, over 185 million working days are lost each year due to sickness or injury, with stress, anxiety, depression, and musculoskeletal pain among the leading causes. Corporate wellbeing programmes are now recognised as a critical investment in employee engagement and organisational performance. Our mission is to help businesses reduce absenteeism and improve retention through evidence-based workplace wellness strategies that deliver measurable outcomes.

Our Expertise

Thames Health and Wellness bring together versatile backgrounds with health and movement expertise with a vision to converge Western medical practise and Eastern holistic traditions. Our 20 years of global health and movement experience is driven by the analytical data-based strategies.

Our Approach

We combine clinical rigour with holistic health principles. By integrating objective data with restorative practices, we create occupational health and wellbeing programmes that are measurable, meaningful, and sustainable. Our approach enables employees to move better, feel calmer, and perform at their best while reducing absenteeism and long-term health risks.
